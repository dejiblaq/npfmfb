About NPF MICROFINANCE BANK PLC

NPF Microfinance Bank (formerly NPF Community Bank Ltd) was incorporated on 19th May 1993 as a Limited Liability Company under the Companies and Allied Matters Act Cap C20LFN 2014. The Bank provides Banking services to both Serving and Retired Officers and Men of the Nigeria Police Force, its ancillary institutions and the general public.
